How are you feeling?

This is a self-assessment quiz for your mental wellbeing. The quiz is not a medical assessment - if you are worried about your mental health or find you in an unhealthy mental state you should turn to professional help. It is only for pointing you in the right direction.
It is also suggested that you write down your answers on a piece of paper. If you choose "not at all", write down A. If you choose "several days", write down B. If you choose "more than half the days", write down C. If you choose "everyday", write down D.

How many A, B, C, and D did you get?

If you mostly get A or B, Congratulate! You are taking good care of your mental wellbeing.
More C or D indicate that you need to pay more attention to your mental health. It is suggested that you take close look at our advice page.
